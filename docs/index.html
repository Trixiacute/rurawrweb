üìÑ Complete Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Discord bot. By using the bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Information:

  • Discord User ID
  • Last.fm username (when provided)
  • Command preferences
  • Feature usage patterns

2.2 Server Information:

  • Server ID
  • Channel IDs for specific features
  • Server prefix settings
  • Feature configurations

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect this information to:

  • Connect your Discord account with your Last.fm profile
  • Provide personalized music recommendations and statistics
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve the bot based on usage patterns
  • Ensure proper functioning of all features

4. Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored securely and is only accessible to bot developers for maintenance and support purposes. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except when necessary for the functioning of our services (e.g., API requests to Last.fm or Discord). Your Last.fm username is only used to retrieve your public music data from Last.fm.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of specific features

You can manage these rights using the !datadelete command within Discord.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your data or stop using the bot, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the bot and on our website. Continued use of the bot after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or data practices, please contact us through our support server or via GitHub issues.

Here's how you can delete your data from our services.

Using the Deletion Command

Use !datadelete in any channel where the bot can read and respond to your messages. This command will guide you through the data deletion process.

What Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we remove:

  • Your Last.fm username
  • Rich presence settings
  • Custom preferences
  • Any other personal identifiers

Deletion Timeframe

Your data will be deleted immediately from our active systems. Any backups containing your data will be purged within 30 days.

Important Note

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You'll need to set up your preferences again if you want to use the bot in the future.
